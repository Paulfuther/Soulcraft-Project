The Platform: Due Diligence Made Visible

Compliance, documentation, and communication — built from real field experience.

What it does

The platform turns operational work into clear, auditable records. Document incidents, complete checklists with photos, trigger notifications, and export proof — all in one place.

Modules
  • Incident forms & photo uploads → PDF
  • Checklists (dynamic items, N/A handling)
  • Employee files & DocuSign templates
  • Store logs (salt logs, pump-outs, shifts)
  • Search across records (natural language)
Automations
  • Event-driven email/SMS (Twilio, SendGrid)
  • Scheduled reports (Celery + Beat)
  • Webhook integrations (DocuSign)
  • Cloud storage (S3-compatible)
  • PDF generation & monthly summaries

Why it helps

  • Proves due diligence: time-stamped records with attachments and signatures.
  • Saves time: fewer emails, fewer spreadsheets, less chasing.
  • Standardizes: consistent process across locations and teams.
  • Built by practitioners: workflows shaped by field realities.
